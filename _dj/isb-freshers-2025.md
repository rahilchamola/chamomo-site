My first proper set at ISB. 90 minutes, ~400 people, and the kind of energy you can't plan for.

## The Set

Started with deep house to let the room settle, then built through progressive Bollywood remixes into a peak-hour electronic block. The transition from the chill opener into the first big drop was the moment the room locked in.

## Lessons

Reading a room of MBA students is its own skill. High energy but impatient — you have about 90 seconds to prove you're worth paying attention to. The opening track matters more than any other decision in the set.

## What Worked

The Bollywood-electronic mashup section killed. Pre-planned transitions that felt spontaneous. Ending on a high instead of fading out.

## What I'd Change

Brought the energy up too fast in the middle. Should have held the tension longer before the main peak. Also need better monitoring — the booth speakers were fighting the mains.
