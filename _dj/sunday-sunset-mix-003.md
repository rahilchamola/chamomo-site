## Series Concept

Sunday Sunset is a weekly mix series designed for the golden hour — that 5-7 PM window where the week is truly over and the evening hasn't started yet. The energy target is "pleasant anticipation."

## This Week's Theme

Went heavier on the indie dance side this week. Lots of guitar-driven grooves with electronic production underneath. Think less Ibiza, more rooftop bar in Goa.

## Production Notes

Recorded in one take on a Sunday afternoon. Used two channels plus a loop deck for some layered transitions. Mixed in Traktor Pro 3 with an external audio interface.

The goal with these mixes is imperfection — they should sound like a DJ having fun, not a studio production. Flaws are features.

## Tracklist

15 tracks, all within the 110-118 BPM range. Key-matched but with some intentional harmonic tension in the middle section to keep it from feeling too predictable.
