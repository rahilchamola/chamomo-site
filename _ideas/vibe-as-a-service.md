Event organizers hire DJs to "play music." But music is just one input to the actual output they want: a specific emotional atmosphere.

What if instead of selling DJ services, you sold **vibe design** — a package that includes music curation, lighting direction, scent selection, temperature recommendations, and crowd flow architecture?

This is basically what high-end event designers do, but at 10x the price point and mostly for weddings/corporate. There's a gap in the market for college events, house parties, and small venues.

**The minimum viable vibe:** A curated Spotify playlist + a lighting color scheme guide + a 1-page "energy map" for the event timeline. Charge ₹5,000 instead of ₹25,000 for a full DJ. Scale through templates.

Probably too weird to actually build. Filing under "revisit in 2027."
