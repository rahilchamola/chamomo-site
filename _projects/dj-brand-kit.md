## The Need

Every gig needs promotional material: Instagram stories, flyers, post-event recaps. Without a system, each piece is designed from scratch. With a brand kit, it's fill-in-the-blanks.

## Components

- **Logo system** — primary mark, wordmark, icon, and DJ tag variations
- **Color palette** — dark primary with accent rotation per event type
- **Flyer templates** — three formats (story, square, landscape) with swappable backgrounds
- **Social templates** — set announcement, tracklist reveal, recap carousel
- **Motion presets** — After Effects templates for looping visuals during sets

## Status

Logo and color system finalized. Flyer templates at 80%. Social templates in progress. Motion presets backlogged.
