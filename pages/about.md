I'm Rahil Chamola — most people know me as Chamomo.

I work at the intersection of AI and creativity, building things that make technology feel human and art feel like technology. Currently doing my MBA at ISB (PGP '26), where I'm focused on product strategy and AI-first thinking.

## The Remix

I've always been someone who connects dots across domains. Whether it's building AI products, DJing a set that reads the room, or designing systems that actually work — the throughline is the same: take existing pieces, understand them deeply, and remix them into something that clicks.

## What I'm Building

Right now I'm exploring how AI can be a creative medium, not just a productivity tool. I think the most interesting work happens when you treat technology as an instrument — something you play, not just something you use.

## DJ Life

Music isn't a side project — it's how I think. DJing taught me about reading rooms, building energy, creating moments. Those skills translate directly into product thinking and storytelling. I perform regularly and I'm building a content brand around my sets.
