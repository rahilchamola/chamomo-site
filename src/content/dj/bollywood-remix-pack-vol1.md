## Why This Exists

DJing in India means you *will* play Bollywood at some point. The challenge: most Bollywood tracks aren't built for DJ use. Odd phrase lengths, no clear mix-in points, tempo shifts mid-song.

This pack takes 12 classic Bollywood tracks and rebuilds them for club play while keeping the melodies and vocal hooks that people know and love.

## What I Did to Each Track

- **Extended intros:** 16-32 bar intros with a filtered or instrumental version of the hook, giving DJs time to mix in smoothly
- **Quantized BPM:** locked each track to a consistent BPM (128 or 130, depending on the original)
- **DJ-friendly structure:** intro → build → drop → breakdown → drop → outro, following standard EDM phrase structure
- **Clean stems:** isolated vocals and melodic elements for layering over other tracks

## Reception

Shared with a few DJ friends at ISB. Three of them used tracks from this pack in their next sets. The highest compliment: "I played it and people didn't even realize it was a remix until the drop."
