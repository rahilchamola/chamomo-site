## The Vibe

Valentine's night, open-air terrace, fairy lights, couples and friend groups mixing. The brief was simple: keep it warm, keep it groovy, don't make anyone feel lonely.

## Set Structure

Started at 118 BPM with some organic house — percussion-forward, vocal samples, no hard drops. Slowly built to 122 over the first 40 minutes. Dropped a reworked Bollywood vocal over a deep house beat at the one-hour mark — that was the peak moment. Brought it back down to close with some ambient-adjacent deep house.

## Tracklist Highlights

- Opened with a slowed-down edit of a classic Hindi melody over a four-on-the-floor groove
- Mid-set featured a 12-minute blend through three tracks in the same key — Cmaj progression
- Closed with a 6-minute ambient outro that let the crowd wind down naturally

## What Worked

The extended blends. This crowd didn't want surprises — they wanted to settle into a groove and stay there. The longest blend was nearly 4 minutes, and it was the most praised moment of the night.

## What I'd Change

Started too quiet. The sound system on the terrace needed more low-end push from the beginning. Took 20 minutes to get the bass balance right.
